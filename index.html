Voices from the Communities We Serve

"I have always wanted to contribute to child welfare, but I wasn't sure where my money would go. Bal Raksha Bharat’s transparency is incredible. I receive regular updates on how my donation is helping children in need. Plus, the 80G tax exemption process was seamless. It feels great to save tax while saving lives."

Ramesh Kumar

Parent, Alwar District, Rajasthan

Ashish Singh

Parent,Gurgaon,Haryana
